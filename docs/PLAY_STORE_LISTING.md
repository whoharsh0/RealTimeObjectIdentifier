## Full description

**Object Identifier** turns your phone camera into a smart scanner that recognizes everyday objects instantly — with no subscription, no account, and no internet required.

Point your camera at anything around you and see live labels with confidence scores. Perfect for learning, accessibility, curiosity, and quick identification on the go.

**Why Object Identifier?**
- **100% free forever** — no limits, no paywalls, no ads
- **Works offline** — AI runs entirely on your device
- **Private** — your camera never uploads photos to the cloud
- **Fast** — real-time detection with bounding boxes
- **Customizable** — adjust confidence threshold and max detections in Settings

**Features**
- Live camera object detection
- Bounding boxes with labels (e.g. "cup 87%")
- Front / rear camera switch
- Adjustable confidence threshold
- Control how many objects are shown
- Toggle confidence percentage on labels
- Dark, modern scanner UI

**Requirements**
- Android 8.0 (API 26) or higher
- Camera permission

**Privacy**
Object Identifier does not collect, store, or transmit personal data. All processing happens locally on your device.

Built with Kotlin, Jetpack Compose, CameraX, and TensorFlow Lite.
