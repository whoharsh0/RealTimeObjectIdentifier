# Privacy Policy — Object Identifier

**Last updated:** June 24, 2026  
**App name:** Object Identifier  
**Package:** com.rudra.objectidentifier  
**Developer:** RUDRA (sharmarudra095@gmail.com)

## Summary

Object Identifier does **not** collect, store, or share your personal data. The app works entirely on your device.

## Information we do NOT collect

- Name, email, or account information (no accounts exist)
- Photos or video from your camera
- Location data
- Contacts, files, or browsing history
- Advertising identifiers for tracking
- Analytics or crash reporting tied to your identity

## Camera usage

The app requests camera permission solely to show a live preview and run on-device object detection. Camera frames are processed in memory on your phone and are **never uploaded** to any server.

## On-device processing

Object detection uses TensorFlow Lite running locally. No cloud AI services (OpenAI, Google Vision, etc.) are used.

## Local storage

The app saves only your **Settings preferences** on device using Android DataStore:
- Confidence threshold
- Max detections
- Show/hide confidence percentage
- Whether onboarding was shown

These settings never leave your device.

## Internet access

The app does not require internet for core functionality. No data is transmitted when you use object detection.

## Children's privacy

The app does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children under 13.

## Third-party libraries

The app uses open-source libraries (Android Jetpack, CameraX, TensorFlow Lite) that operate locally. No third party receives your camera data.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this policy if the app changes. Updates will be posted in the app repository.
